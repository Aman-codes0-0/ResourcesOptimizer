# TeamOptimizer - Module Guide

Here is an explanation of each module visible in the sidebar:

## 1. Dashboard
**Overview**: The central command center of the application.
**Functionality**: Displays high-level metrics (Total Resources, Active Projects, Bench Strength), utilization charts, and real-time alerts. It gives you a quick snapshot of your organization's health.

## 2. Resources
**Overview**: The master database of all employees and contractors.
**Functionality**:
- View a list of all resources with their roles, skills, and status.
- Add new resources or edit existing profiles.
- Filter resources by skill or availability.

## 3. Projects
**Overview**: Management of all active and planned initiatives.
**Functionality**:
- Track project timelines, budgets, and priority.
- View required skills for each project.
- Monitor project status (Planning, Active, Completed).

## 4. Allocations
**Overview**: Managing the assignment of people to projects.
**Functionality**:
- Visual Gantt-chart style view of who is working on what.
- Assign resources to projects for specific dates and utilization percentages.
- Auto-optimize allocations to reduce bench time.

## 5. Analytics
**Overview**: Deep dive into data trends.
**Functionality**:
- **Utilization Trends**: See how busy your team is over time.
- **Skill Demand**: Identify which skills are most requested vs. available.
- **Bench Analysis**:  Track non-billable time to improve efficiency.

## 6. AI Insights
**Overview**: Intelligent recommendations powered by the system's engine.
**Functionality**:
- **Smart Staffing**: Get AI suggestions for the best person for a role.
- **Churn Risk**: Identify employees at risk of leaving based on utilization patterns.
- **Team Composition**: Suggestions for balancing teams with complementary skills.

## 7. Requests (Management)
**Overview**: Workflow for resource requisitions.
**Functionality**:
- Managers can submit requests for new resources.
- Admins can review, approve, or reject these requests.
- Tracks the history of all staffing requests.

## 8. Cost Analysis
**Overview**: Financial tracking for projects.
**Functionality**:
- **Budget vs. Actuals**: Compare planned budget against real resource costs.
- **Overrun Alerts**: Highlights projects that are exceeding their financial limits.
- **Cost Breakdown**: detailed view of costs by project or department.

## 9. Reports
**Overview**: Document generation and data export.
**Functionality**:
- **PDF Reports**: Generate executive summaries (e.g., Monthly Utilization Report).
- **Data Export**: Download raw data in Excel or CSV format for external analysis.

## 10. Settings (System)
**Overview**: Configuration of the application.
**Functionality**:
- **Profile**: Update your user details.
- **Users & Roles**: Manage system access (Admin/Manager/Viewer).
- **Integrations**: Connect with JIRA, Slack, or Google Calendar.
